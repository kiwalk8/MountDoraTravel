Mount Dora, Florida

AWAITS YOU

Eat

Check out Mount Dora's many delicious restaurants. Cuisine ranges from authentic Cuban to brick oven pizza. Enjoy the breeze with outdoor seating at most locations, as well as mutliple opportunities for live music throughout the weekends!

Drink

Grab a drink from multiple walk-up locations including traditional and mimosa bars or grab a coffee while you explore all that Mount Dora has to offer.

Shop

Spend your day shopping away at Mount Dora's downtown area. Filled with small local shops, you can find just about anything. Say hello to local artists and crafters, as well as the resident finches at the Nest!

Explore

Check out the lighthouse, take a walk on the boardwalk and wave to all the alligators and diving birds, rent a jet ski or a see-through kayak, or take a small roadtrip to nearby Daytona Beach.
